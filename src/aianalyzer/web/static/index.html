What do planning and control mean?

Planning axis

Measures whether you think before you type. Computed from how often your prompts use planner-words ("plan", "design", "first"), mention tests or specs, create explicit TODOs, ask questions instead of giving orders, and how long you pause between the AI's reply and your next prompt.

+ = Planner (you specify first)  ·  − = Doer (you act first)

Control axis

Measures how hands-on you stay while the AI is working. Computed from how many distinct tools you invoke, files you touch per turn, how often you accept the AI's first response without revisions (inverted), and how often you have to retry.

+ = Hands-on (you steer the tools)  ·  − = Delegator (you let the AI run)

Hands-on → ← Delegator ↑ Planner ↓ Doer Pilot Architect Vibe Coder Tinkerer
You sit between the four archetypes on the (control, planning) plane. The closer you are to a corner, the purer that archetype.
